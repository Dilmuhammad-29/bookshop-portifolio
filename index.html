    Delivery

    We work without holidays and weekends! Residents of Kyiv can receive their order on the day it is placed. Customers from other cities of Ukraine can receive their order within 1-5 days, depending on the location of the city and the chosen delivery method. Orders over 1000 UAH are delivered free*. You can see the available methods, exact delivery times and costs during checkout in the order basket, after selecting the delivery city.
